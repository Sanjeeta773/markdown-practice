# Delicious Mo:Mo Recipe

## Why I Love Mo:Mo

Mo:Mo is **a popular dumpling dish** that is both delicious and fun to make.  
It is _a favorite street food_ in Nepal.  
Making Mo:Mo at home is ***an enjoyable experience for friends and family***.

### Ingredients Needed

To prepare Mo:Mo, you will need:

- All-purpose flour
- Ground meat (chicken or pork)
- Vegetables (cabbage, carrot, onion)
- Garlic and ginger
- Spices

---

## Steps to Make Mo:Mo

1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour and water, then knead until smooth.
2. Prepare the filling with meat, vegetables, and seasonings.
3. Roll out the dough and cut into small circles.
4. Place filling in each circle and fold into dumplings.
5. Steam the Mo:Mo for 10-15 minutes.
6. Serve with spicy dipping sauce.
